Hello! I am a PhD candidate in computer science at UC Berkeley. I'm extremely fortunate to be coadvised by [Ben Recht](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~brecht/){:target="_blank"} and [Nika Haghtalab](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~nika/){:target="_blank"}, and to be supported by a NSF GRFP. 

My recent work concerns how we might learn from, and give voice to, members of the public regarding AI (especially regarding 'societal impacts'); this includes, e.g., [post-deployment evaluations via individual reporting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.18133){:target="_blank"} or [using social media data as early signals for impact](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05750){:target="_blank"}.
Feel free to reach out if you're thinking about anything related! 

Beyond my academic work, I am a cofounder of [Reboot](https://reboothq.substack.com/about){:target="_blank"} and have edited many issues of [Kernel](https://kernelmag.io/){:target="_blank"}, our annual(ish) print magazine. 
I've also thought a lot and have feelings about [Chinese dance](https://letterstomyfriends.substack.com/p/mirrors){:target="_blank"}, [~ alignment ~](https://joinreboot.org/p/alignment){:target="_blank"}, and [doing Real creative work](https://letterstomyfriends.substack.com/p/on-doing-real-work){:target="_blank"} (research or otherwise).
